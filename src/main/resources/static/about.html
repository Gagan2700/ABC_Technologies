About ABC Technologies

Fourteen years of helping organizations modernize their infrastructure, one dependable engagement at a time.

Our History

Built by engineers who wanted to do the work differently

ABC Technologies was founded in 2011 by a small group of infrastructure engineers who had grown frustrated with the disconnect between IT vendors and the businesses they served. The founding team set out to build a consultancy where technical recommendations were grounded in a client's real environment, not a standard package of services.

What began as a four-person systems administration practice in Austin has grown into a 165-person organization spanning cloud architecture, DevOps engineering, application development, and cybersecurity. Throughout that growth, we have kept our original operating principle: every engagement begins with an honest technical assessment, not a sales pitch.

2011

Company founded in Austin, Texas

Started as a systems administration and infrastructure support practice serving local mid-market businesses.

2015

Cloud practice established

Formed a dedicated cloud architecture team as clients began migrating on-premises workloads to AWS and Azure.

2018

DevOps and Chicago office launch

Opened a second delivery center in Chicago and introduced formal DevOps consulting services, including CI/CD and containerization.

2021

Cybersecurity division added

Built a dedicated security practice offering penetration testing and managed monitoring for regulated industries.

2024

Pune delivery center opened

Expanded international delivery capacity to provide extended-hours support coverage for enterprise clients.

Our Mission

To give organizations technology infrastructure that is stable, secure, and understandable — delivered by engineers who remain accountable for the systems they build.

Our Vision

To be the technology partner mid-size and enterprise organizations turn to first — known for straightforward guidance and infrastructure that holds up under real-world conditions.

What Guides Us

Core Values
