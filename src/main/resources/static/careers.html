Careers at ABC Technologies

Join a team of engineers who are given real ownership over the systems they build.

Our Culture

Engineers stay close to the client, and to each other

At ABC Technologies, project teams are small by design, typically three to six engineers, so individual contributions are visible and decisions move quickly. We favor direct communication over layers of approval, and every engineer is expected to speak with clients directly rather than working through a separate account team.

We also invest in internal knowledge sharing. Every delivery center runs a weekly technical session where engineers present recent problems they have solved, which has become one of the more effective ways new hires ramp up on our tools and standards.

Small, Accountable Teams

Most projects are staffed with three to six engineers who stay on the account from scoping through support.

Direct Client Exposure

Engineers, not just account managers, regularly join client calls and status reviews.
